Koala Guard – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Summary

Koala Guard is a privacy-protection utility that runs entirely on your Mac. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no servers, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

Camera Use

Koala Guard accesses the built-in or connected camera only to detect the number of human faces in front of your Mac in real time. This face-count detection happens entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework. No image, video, or biometric data is ever stored, saved to disk, or transmitted off your device.

Camera frames are processed in memory and immediately discarded.

Data Collection

Koala Guard collects zero personal data. Specifically:

  • No user identifiers, accounts, or sign-ins
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, calendars, or other personal content
  • No camera images, videos, or face data leave your Mac

Settings & Preferences

Your Koala Guard settings (such as which apps to protect and detection thresholds) are stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. They never leave your device.

Third Parties

Koala Guard does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children

Koala Guard is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.
