Koala Guard – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Summary

Koala Guard is a privacy-protection utility that runs entirely on your Mac. All processing, including all face detection and face recognition, happens on-device. Koala Guard has no servers, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs, and nothing — including face data — is ever transmitted off your Mac or shared with anyone.

Camera Use

Koala Guard accesses the built-in or connected camera only to detect faces in front of your Mac and to recognize your own enrolled face, in real time, using Apple's on-device Vision framework. Live camera frames are processed in memory and immediately discarded. No image or video is ever saved to disk or transmitted off your device.

Face Data

This section explains exactly how Koala Guard handles face data.

  • What is collected: When you choose to enroll a face ("Add My Face" / "Trusted Faces"), Koala Guard generates a numerical feature print of that face using Apple's Vision framework (VNFeaturePrintObservation), plus a small thumbnail image of the face used only to show you which face you enrolled. No photographs or video of you are otherwise stored. If you never enroll a face, no face data is stored at all.
  • How it is used: The stored feature print is used solely, on your Mac, to tell you apart from other people in front of the camera. When monitoring is on, each face seen is compared on-device against your enrolled face; if a face that is not yours appears, Koala Guard hides or covers your protected apps and restores them when only you remain. The face data is used for no other purpose.
  • Sharing / third parties: Face data is never shared with us or any third party, and is never uploaded to any server. There are no third-party SDKs or analytics. It stays on your Mac.
  • Where it is stored: Enrolled face data is stored only in Koala Guard's local Application Support folder on your Mac.
  • How long it is retained: Enrolled face data is kept on your Mac until you delete the trusted face inside the app or uninstall Koala Guard, at which point it is removed. You can delete any enrolled face at any time from the Trusted Faces screen.

Data Collection

Apart from the on-device face data described above, Koala Guard collects zero personal data. Specifically:

  • No user identifiers, accounts, or sign-ins
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, calendars, or other personal content
  • No camera images, videos, or face data ever leave your Mac

Settings & Preferences

Your Koala Guard settings (such as which apps to protect and detection thresholds) are stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. They never leave your device.

Third Parties

Koala Guard does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children

Koala Guard is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.
